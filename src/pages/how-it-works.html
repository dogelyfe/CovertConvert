How CovertConvert Works

Unlike most "free" online converters, CovertConvert processes your images entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device. Here's how.

The Traditional Way (Risky)

  1. 1. You upload your photo to their server
  2. 2. Their server processes and stores your file
  3. 3. You download the result
  4. 4. Your original file may remain on their servers indefinitely

The problem: You have no control over what happens to your photos. They could be stored, analyzed, or used for AI training without your knowledge.

The CovertConvert Way (Private)

  1. 1. You drop your file onto CovertConvert
  2. 2. Your browser reads the file locally (no upload)
  3. 3. WebAssembly code converts the image using your CPU
  4. 4. The converted file is saved directly to your Downloads

The result: Your files never touch our servers. We couldn't see them if we wanted to.

The Technical Details

Client-Side Processing

All image decoding and encoding happens in your browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly (WASM). The heavy lifting is done by your device's CPU, not our servers.

WebAssembly Codecs

For formats like HEIC and AVIF, we use optimized WebAssembly libraries (compiled from C/C++) that run at near-native speed in your browser. These are only loaded when needed.

Canvas API

For common formats like PNG, WebP, and GIF, we use your browser's built-in Canvas API. It's fast, efficient, and requires no additional code to download.

Blob URLs

When you "download" your converted file, it's not downloading from our server. The browser creates a temporary URL pointing to data in memory, then saves it to your Downloads folder.

Verify It Yourself

Don't just take our word for it. Here's how to verify that your files aren't being uploaded:

  1. 1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12 or right-click → Inspect)
  2. 2. Go to the Network tab
  3. 3. Convert a file and watch the network requests
  4. 4. You'll see no uploads — just the initial page load and lazy-loaded scripts

You can even disconnect from the internet after the page loads. Conversion will still work.

CovertConvert proves that you can have a fast, free tool without sacrificing privacy. Your photos are your business — not ours.
