What is HEIC?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's default photo format since iOS 11. It produces files roughly half the size of JPEG while maintaining the same quality — great for saving storage on your iPhone.

The downside? Many apps, websites, and Windows computers don't support HEIC natively. That's where CovertConvert comes in — convert to JPG instantly without uploading your photos anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to convert HEIC to JPG online?

Most online converters upload your files to their servers. CovertConvert is different — your files never leave your device. Conversion happens entirely in your browser using your computer's processing power. We literally cannot see your files.

Why are my iPhone photos HEIC?

Apple uses HEIC because it produces smaller file sizes than JPEG while maintaining quality. This saves storage space on your iPhone. You can change this in Settings → Camera → Formats → Most Compatible, but existing photos will remain HEIC.

Will converting HEIC to JPG lose quality?

There's minimal quality loss when converting HEIC to JPG. CovertConvert uses high-quality encoding (92% JPEG quality) to preserve as much detail as possible. For lossless conversion, choose PNG output instead.

Can I convert multiple HEIC files at once?

Yes! Drop as many HEIC files as you want. They'll be converted one by one and delivered as a single ZIP file for easy download.
