Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to convert images online?

Most online converters upload your files to their servers. CovertConvert is different — your files never leave your device. Conversion happens entirely in your browser using your computer's processing power. We literally cannot see your files.

What formats can I convert?

CovertConvert supports HEIC/HEIF (iPhone photos), WebP, PNG, AVIF, TIFF, BMP, and GIF. You can convert any of these to either JPEG or PNG format.

Why are my iPhone photos HEIC?

Apple uses HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) because it produces smaller file sizes than JPEG while maintaining quality. However, many apps and Windows computers don't support HEIC natively, which is why you might need to convert them.

Is there a file size or quantity limit?

There's no artificial limit — convert as many files as your browser can handle. Since processing happens on your device, very large batches (50+ large photos) might slow things down. If that happens, just convert in smaller batches.
