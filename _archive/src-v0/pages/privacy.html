Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

The Short Version

Your files never leave your device. We don't upload, store, process, or even see the images you convert. All conversion happens locally in your browser.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your images or files (they never leave your device)
  • File names, sizes, or metadata
  • Personal information from your photos
  • Accounts or registration data (there's no signup)

What We May Collect

To keep the site running and understand general usage patterns, we may collect:

  • Analytics data: Page views, general geographic region, device type, browser type. This is aggregate data that doesn't identify you personally.
  • Ad data: If we display ads, ad networks may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out via your browser settings or ad preferences.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For hosting and CDN. They may collect standard web server logs.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying ads. Google's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • CDN libraries: We load JavaScript libraries (like JSZip, libheif) from CDNs. These are static files with no tracking.

Cookies

CovertConvert itself doesn't set cookies. However, third-party services (analytics, ads) may set cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How to Verify Our Claims

We encourage you to verify that your files aren't being uploaded:

  1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to the Network tab
  3. Convert a file and watch for upload requests
  4. You'll see none — conversion is 100% local

You can even disconnect from the internet after the page loads. Conversion will still work.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Our core commitment — that your files never leave your device — won't change.
